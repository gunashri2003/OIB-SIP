About Us

Welcome to Shri's Nrithyalaya, where dance is celebrated with passion and grace. Our school is devoted to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Bharatanatyam in the "Vazhuvoor" style. Students have graced temples including Chidambaram, Malaysia Murugan Temple, Thanjavur Periya Kovil, Trivandrum, Guruvayur Temple, and more, showcasing their artistry.
